ABOUT ATM'25

Achariya College of Engineering Technology, Puducherry, is delighted to host ATM 25, a National Level Technical Symposium, on October 10, 2025. This annual technical fest is designed to encourage innovation, creativity, and collaboration among young engineers and technologists. It provides a unique platform for students to showcase their technical skills, share knowledge, and gain insights into emerging trends in engineering and technology.

ATM 25 will feature a vibrant mix of events including paper presentations, project expos, technical quizzes, hackathons, and expert talks by industry leaders. These sessions are carefully curated to enhance the problem-solving abilities, technical expertise, and research orientation of participants. In addition to the technical programs, the symposium also offers engaging cultural and fun events, ensuring a holistic and memorable experience for all attendees.

By bringing together students, academicians, and professionals from across the country, ATM 25 aims to foster an environment of learning, networking, and innovation. Participants will not only have the chance to compete and win recognition but also to build meaningful connections and expand their horizons. With its commitment to excellence, Achariya College of Engineering Technology looks forward to making ATM 25 a grand success and a celebration of engineering brilliance.

ABOUT ACET

Achariya College of Engineering Technology, which was established in 2010, has spent over a decade dedicated to nurturing the academic and professional growth of its students and staff. Over the years, the institution has evolved into a hub of Innovation and Excellence, continually adapting to the ever-changing demands of the Engineering field. The college has built a strong reputation for its commitment to high-quality Education, state-of-the-art Facilities, and a supportive Learning Environment. With a focus on both theoretical Knowledge and practical Skills, it has successfully equipped its graduates with the tools needed to excel in their careers. The college’s success can be attributed to its dedicated Faculty, progressive Curriculum, and emphasis on Research and Development. As it moves forward, Achariya College of Engineering Technology remains steadfast in its mission to empower students and contribute to the advancement of Technology and Society.

Achariya Group of Institutions, celebrating its 25th glorious year of excellence in education, proudly presents Achariya Tech Meet – ATM 25, a National Level Technical Symposium, hosted by Achariya College of Engineering Technology, Puducherry, on October 10, 2025.
